White Water Rafting

Our Mission & Creed

Our purpose is to run rivers boldly and share the thrill of moving water with every adventurer. Our mission is to guide each trip with courage, care, and a love for the wild, ensuring fun, safety, and unforgettable memories. Our creed is to respect the river, trust the team, paddle with heart, and leave nature better than we found it. Our motto is simple: paddle hard, laugh loud, and ride the river together.

History

Founded many seasons ago by a small group of river-loving guides, our company began with a simple dream: to share the excitement and beauty of whitewater rafting with others. What started as a few rafts and a passion for adventure has grown into a trusted outfitter known for teamwork, safety, and unforgettable river experiences.

Whitewater rafting began as Native peoples’ river travel long before recreation. Modern rafting started in the 1950s with inflatable rafts. Today rafting is a global adventure sport with graded river classes, safety gear, and guided expeditions.
