Nigeria

Nigeria is a country in West Africa and is often called the Giant of Africa because of its large population and influence. It is the most populous country in Africa, with over 200 million people. Nigeria has 36 states and the Federal Capital Territory, Abuja, which is the capital. The country is culturally diverse, with over 250 ethnic groups and more than 500 languages, including Hausa, Yoruba, and Igbo. English is the official language. Nigeria is known for its rich culture, Afrobeats music, Nollywood films, and strong entrepreneurial spirit.
